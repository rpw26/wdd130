Body Types

Most people are a mix, but these categories can help you choose workouts that fit you better. Pick the one that matches you the closest.

Ectomorph (naturally skinny)

Usually slimmer and burns calories fast. Focus on strength training and eating enough.

  • Lift heavier weights
  • Less cardio
  • Rest more between sets

Mesomorph (naturally athletic)

Builds muscle easier. Balanced workouts work well.

  • Mix strength + cardio
  • Train 3–5 days a week
  • Focus on consistency

Endomorph (gains weight easier)

Can build strength but also gains fat easier. Combine lifting with more cardio.

  • Lift consistently
  • More cardio / walking
  • Watch calories
