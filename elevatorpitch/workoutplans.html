Workout Plans

These are simple starter plans. Pick the goal that matches what you want to look like. Repeat the same week for a few weeks and slowly increase weight.

Muscle Gain

  • Lift 4 days a week
  • Focus on squats, bench, rows, deadlifts
  • Low cardio (1–2 days)

Fat Loss

  • Lift 3 days a week
  • Cardio 3 days a week
  • Walk more and stay active

Beginner Plan

  • Full-body workouts 3 days a week
  • Learn form first, then add weight
  • Optional easy cardio on off days
